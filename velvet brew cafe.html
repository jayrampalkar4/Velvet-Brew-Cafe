The Velvet Standard

Founded in 2023, Velvet Brew was born from a desire to slow down time. We believe coffee isn't just a morning ritual; it's an experience.


Our beans are ethically sourced from the highlands of Ethiopia and Colombia, roasted locally to perfection, and brewed with water filtered for the purest taste. Come sit in our velvet armchairs, enjoy the jazz, and let the aroma wrap around you.
